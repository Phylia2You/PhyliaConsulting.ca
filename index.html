Frequently Asked Questions

What to expect before starting a project.

Clear answers about scope, timing, costs, and ongoing support.

What is included in a basic website?

A custom mobile-friendly site, agreed core pages, one standard contact form, essential SEO, testing, domain connection, launch support, and up to two revision rounds.

Why do prices say “starting at”?

The final price depends on the number of pages, content, design complexity, integrations, and revisions. You receive a clear scope and quote before work begins.

Are domain and hosting costs included?

Usually not. We can help register or connect the domain and recommend a suitable hosting setup. Third-party charges are explained before the project begins.

Can forms connect to email or a CRM?

Yes. Forms can send notifications, save structured information, feed a dashboard or CRM, and support approval-first follow-up workflows.

How long does a website take?

A focused project may be built within one to several weeks. Timing depends on content readiness, integrations, feedback, and how quickly approvals are provided.

Do I need monthly maintenance?

No. Maintenance is optional, but recommended if you want routine updates, monitoring, minor changes, and help when forms or integrations need attention.

Do you provide network, Wi-Fi, or voice services?

Yes. We can help with small-business network setup, Wi-Fi planning and troubleshooting, business phone and voice configuration, and provider coordination.

What other IT services are available?

Services can include domains, DNS, business email, Cloudflare, connectivity, business tools, integrations, troubleshooting, documentation, and related technical setup.

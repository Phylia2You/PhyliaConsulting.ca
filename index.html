Frequently Asked Questions

What to expect before starting a project.

What is included in a basic website?

A custom mobile-friendly site, core business pages, contact information, essential SEO setup, testing, launch support, and basic documentation. The exact page count and features are confirmed during scoping.

Are domain and hosting costs included?

Usually not. I can help register or connect the domain and recommend a suitable hosting setup. Any third-party charges are explained before the project begins.

Can forms connect to email or a CRM?

Yes. Forms can send notifications, save structured information, feed a dashboard or CRM, and support approval-first follow-up workflows.

How long does a website take?

A focused small-business website often takes several weeks, depending on the number of pages, content readiness, integrations, and how quickly approvals are provided.

Do I need monthly maintenance?

No. Maintenance is optional, but recommended if you want routine updates, monitoring, minor changes, and someone available when forms or integrations need attention.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes. I can review the current site and recommend whether a focused update or a full rebuild would be more practical.

Do you build online stores?

Small ecommerce projects can be considered. The platform, products, payments, shipping, taxes, and ongoing management requirements need to be reviewed before quoting.

Do you provide other IT services?

Yes, where they connect naturally to the website or business setup. This can include domains, DNS, business email, Cloudflare, forms, integrations, connectivity, troubleshooting, and practical tool setup.
